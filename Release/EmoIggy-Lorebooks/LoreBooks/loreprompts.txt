Description
Most of the Golems were constructed in Harmattan and in the Electric Lab in Abrassar during the initial attack from The Scourge which struck Aurai around 100 years ago. The Golems were specifically designed to match the Scourge in combat, which can be seen in the visual resemblances as well as similar combat styles.

There are also other types of Golems created in other ways, such as the creations of the Light Mender and Plague Doctor.
These Golems are more semi-mechanical, and are heavily imbued with the magic of the Liches.

Commonly immune to Bleeding and Poison effects
Usually resistant to Decay Decay and vulnerable to Ethereal Ethereal, with some exceptions
